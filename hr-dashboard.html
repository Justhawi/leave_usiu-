Annual Leave Policy

Full-time staff are entitled to 21 working days of annual leave per year, accrued monthly and subject to approval by department heads.

Sick Leave Policy

Staff are entitled to up to 10 working days of sick leave per year, with medical certificate required for absences exceeding 2 days.

10 Days/Year

Maternity Leave Policy

Female staff are entitled to 90 calendar days of maternity leave, payable at full salary subject to statutory requirements.

90 Days
