How It Works

Getting started is simple. Follow these three easy steps to begin managing your leave efficiently.

1

Sign Up / Login

Create your account using your USIU email address or login with existing credentials. The system automatically assigns your role and dashboard.

2

Submit Requests

Fill out a simple form specifying leave type, dates, and reason. Your remaining balance is displayed, and the system validates your request automatically.

3

Track & Monitor

View real-time status updates on your dashboard. Receive notifications when requests are approved. Access your complete history anytime.
